Philippine Red Cross Household Profile Survey
Click a sector to view the questions for that section. Color markers only display when both a sector and question are open. Heatmap will show concentrated areas of an answer when a specific answer is clicked. Use the three buttons to toggle display types on and off. Change the base map with the layer icon in the top-right corner of the map. completed surveys are included and are visible in the current extent.
